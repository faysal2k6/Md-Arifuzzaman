SEC. 01 — ABOUT

Engineering reliability into every plant.

I'm a Graduate Mechanical Engineer based in Barishal, Bangladesh, currently serving as Dy. Manager, Engineering at Opsonin Pharma Ltd. Over the past 10+ years I've moved between tobacco, plastics, and pharmaceutical manufacturing — always landing back on the same work: reading a plant drawing, sourcing the right equipment, and seeing a project through to commissioning.

My most recent focus has been the engineering build-out of a penicillin production facility — coordinating consultants, finalizing layouts and specifications, and managing procurement from comparative supplier evaluation through to installation. I pair that hands-on engineering background with an MBA in Finance, which keeps cost and commercial trade-offs in view alongside the technical ones.
